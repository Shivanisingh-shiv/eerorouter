What You Need for EERO Setup

Before setting up your EERO mesh WiFi system, make sure you have:

  • Your EERO router and any additional EERO beacons or nodes
  • Your EERO account credentials (email and password)
  • The EERO mobile app (available for iOS and Android)
  • A modem with an active internet connection
  • Ethernet cable (included with your EERO)
Pro Tip: For best performance, place your primary EERO near your modem in a central location.

Step-by-Step EERO Setup Guide

1

Download the EERO App

Install the EERO app from the Apple App Store or Google Play Store. This app is essential for setup and management of your EERO system.

2

Connect Your Primary EERO

Connect your main EERO router to your modem using the included Ethernet cable. Plug in the power and wait for the LED to turn white.

3

Add Additional Nodes

Place EERO nodes about 40-50 feet apart throughout your home. Use the app to add each node one at a time following the on-screen instructions.

4

Configure Your Network

Set your network name (SSID) and password. Enable advanced features like guest network, device prioritization, and security options.

Troubleshooting EERO Issues

Common Problems and Solutions

  • No Internet Connection: Check modem connection and restart both modem and EERO
  • Slow Speeds: Run speed tests and optimize node placement
  • Node Offline: Check power and move closer to another node
  • App Connection Issues: Ensure your phone is connected to the EERO network
  • Frequent Disconnects: Check for interference from other electronics
Optimization Tip: For best performance, place EERO nodes in open spaces at waist height, away from large metal objects and electronics that may cause interference.
